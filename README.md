# ☕ Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze coffee shop sales performance, customer purchasing patterns, product trends, and store performance. The dashboard provides business insights that help improve operational efficiency and support strategic decision-making.

---

# 📌 Project Overview

The Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard is designed to monitor sales performance across multiple store locations, product categories, and time periods.

Using interactive visualizations, the dashboard enables managers to identify top-selling products, analyze customer buying behavior, compare store performance, and optimize daily business operations.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Coffee shop businesses generate thousands of transactions every month.

Without a centralized dashboard, it becomes difficult to:

- Track daily sales
- Monitor store performance
- Identify best-selling products
- Analyze customer purchasing patterns
- Compare weekday and weekend sales
- Optimize staffing and inventory

This dashboard transforms raw transactional data into actionable business insights.

# 💡 Key Business Insights

- Generated **$157K** in total sales during May 2023.
- Processed **33,527 customer orders**.
- Sold **48,233 products**.
- Weekday sales contribute approximately **74%** of total revenue.
- Hell's Kitchen recorded the highest store sales.
- Coffee is the highest-selling product category.
- Barista Espresso is the top-performing product.
- Peak customer activity occurs during the morning hours.
